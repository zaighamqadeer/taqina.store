Privacy Policy

Taqina
Last updated: January 2025

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Important: This privacy policy applies to all Taqina applications, including Labify and future medical AI applications.

1. Information We Collect

App Usage Analytics

  • Anonymous app usage statistics through Firebase Analytics
  • Device information for advertising purposes (Google AdMob)
  • App performance and crash reports
  • Feature usage patterns (anonymized)

What We DON'T Collect

  • Personal identifiable information (PII)
  • User accounts or profiles
  • Contact information
  • Location data
  • Medical history or health records

2. Medical Images and Data

Zero Storage Policy: Your medical images are NEVER saved or stored on our servers.

Image Processing

  • Images are processed temporarily in secure cloud environment
  • All images are automatically deleted immediately after analysis
  • No backup copies are created
  • Processing happens in real-time with no persistent storage

Data Transmission

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/SSL
  • Secure connection to Google Cloud Platform for processing
  • No data is transmitted to third parties except for processing

3. Third-Party Services

Firebase Analytics

  • Collects anonymous usage statistics
  • Helps improve app performance and user experience
  • No personally identifiable information collected

Google AdMob

  • Serves advertisements within the app
  • Collects device information for ad targeting
  • May use cookies and similar technologies
  • Users can opt-out of personalized ads in device settings

Google Cloud Platform

  • Processes medical images for OCR and analysis
  • Operates under strict security and privacy standards
  • Data is processed and immediately deleted

4. Data Security

Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure cloud processing environment
  • No persistent storage of sensitive information
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Compliance with industry standard security practices

Data Protection

  • No personal health information (PHI) is stored
  • All processing is temporary and ephemeral
  • Automatic deletion of all uploaded content

5. Your Rights and Choices

User Rights

  • Right to request information about data collection
  • Right to request deletion of any stored data
  • Right to opt-out of analytics tracking
  • Right to disable personalized advertisements

Data Control

  • No account required - no data tied to user identity
  • Complete control over what images you upload
  • Option to use the app without providing any personal information

6. Children's Privacy

Our applications are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. International Data Transfers

Data processing may occur on servers located outside your country of residence. We ensure that any international data transfers comply with applicable privacy laws and maintain appropriate security measures.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Modified" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Notification: Significant changes will be announced through app notifications.

9. Legal Basis for Processing

GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

  • Legitimate Interest: App functionality and improvement
  • Consent: Analytics and advertising (where required)
  • Contract: Providing the medical analysis service

10. Data Retention

We follow a strict data minimization approach:

  • Medical Images: Deleted immediately after processing (within minutes)
  • Analytics Data: Retained for up to 26 months (Firebase default)
  • Crash Reports: Retained for up to 90 days
  • Ad Data: Managed by Google AdMob according to their retention policy
