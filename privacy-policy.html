PrepMaster Application - Camera Permission

Camera Usage: PrepMaster uses your device's camera solely for educational purposes.

How We Use Camera Permission

  • Capture study materials, notes, and documents for personal learning
  • Scan textbooks, worksheets, and educational content
  • Take photos of whiteboards, presentations, and study materials
  • Create visual study aids and flashcards
  • Document learning progress and achievements

Camera Data Handling

  • Local Storage: Images are stored locally on your device only
  • No Automatic Upload: Photos are never automatically uploaded to our servers
  • User Control: You decide which images to keep, share, or delete
  • No Background Access: Camera is only accessed when you actively use the feature
  • Permission Control: You can revoke camera permission at any time in device settings

Data Processing

  • Images may be processed locally for text recognition (OCR)
  • If cloud processing is used, images are encrypted and deleted immediately after processing
  • No permanent storage of captured images on external servers
  • All processing is done to enhance your learning experience

Your Privacy: PrepMaster respects your privacy. We never access your camera without your explicit action, and we never store or share your images without your consent.
